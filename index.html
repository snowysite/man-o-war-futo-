Man O War Nigeria

The Man O War Association of Nigeria was established in 1986 as a voluntary, non-political, and non-sectarian organization. It was created to instill discipline, patriotism, and community service in Nigerian youths.

The organization focuses on character building, leadership development, and national integration. It serves as a bridge between the military and civilian populations, promoting peace and unity across Nigeria.

Man O War operates in all 36 states of Nigeria and the Federal Capital Territory, with commands in universities, polytechnics, and communities nationwide.

FUTO Command History

The Man O War FUTO Command was established in 2001 as part of the university's commitment to holistic student development. Since inception, it has trained over 2,000 students in leadership, discipline, and community service.

Our command has consistently been recognized as one of the most active and disciplined units in the South-East region. We have participated in numerous national assignments including election monitoring, disaster relief, and community development projects.

The FUTO Command has produced notable alumni who now serve in various leadership positions across Nigeria, carrying forward the values of integrity, service, and excellence.
